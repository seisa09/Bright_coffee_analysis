OVERVIEW
*In this you will find the coverage of the Bright Coffee sales perfomance during the first half of 2023.

AIM
*This aims at recognising the performance of products in different locations, at differnt times
of day and months to be able to improve on the revenue.

INSIGHTS
*There is a clear growth in sales over the period of 6 months up to +R698 812.
*There are two best performing products, cooffee and tea which contribute to the overall increase in revenue.
*Early mornings are the busiest times of day.
*Hells kitchen is the highest in revenue up to RR236 511,17.
*Budget or low range products have the most quantities sold.

RECOMMENDATIONS
*Ensuring that peak hours have the medium to high inevtory to avoid custumers waiting.
*Creating combos and promotions to attract customers.
*Possibly stopping the slow moving products.
*Driving customer satisfaction by ensuring that the employees are well informed and trained.
